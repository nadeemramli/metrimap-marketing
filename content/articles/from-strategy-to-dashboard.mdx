A good strategy isn't a document — it's a chain of cause and effect. An
objective sets a direction. A hypothesis becomes an action. That action is
supposed to move a metric. And that metric shows up on someone's dashboard.

## Following the thread

The reason strategy so often "doesn't land" is that the thread breaks between
those steps. Canvasm keeps every link on the same map, so you can trace an
action all the way to the number it moved — and see it reflected on the
dashboards your teams already watch.

## Same map, tailored views

Finance sees finance. Marketing sees marketing. Leadership sees everything.
It's the same underlying map, with views tailored to each team.
