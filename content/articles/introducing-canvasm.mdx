Metrics live in dashboards. Strategy lives in decks. Work lives in tickets. And
almost nobody can see the connections between them.

Canvasm is a living map that puts all three on one canvas — so a change to the
work is visibly connected to the number it's meant to move.

## The problem with three separate worlds

When strategy, metrics, and work live in different tools, you lose the thread
between _what we decided_, _what we're doing about it_, and _whether it's
working_. Canvasm keeps that thread visible.

We'll use this space to share how teams map their work, how visibility works
